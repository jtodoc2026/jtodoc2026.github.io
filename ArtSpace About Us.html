About Us <3

Hello there!! Thank you for visiting ArtSpace (＾▽＾)

I'm Johana J. Todoc. I'm 14 years old and the creator of ArtSpace. My interest in art started when I was about 8 years old. I started drawing birthday banners for my family whenever it was their time of the year. I also drew a lot on a sketchbook that was given to me as a gift. However, it wasn’t easy to go into the field of drawing or painting. As I was still a beginner, I didn’t know about the concepts about color theory, anatomy, perspective, etc., which is why I always thought my art was trash and that it would never get any better. This is why I stopped drawing for a while.

During the pandemic, with more frequent access to social media, I saw a bountiful number of art tutorials, tips, tricks on YouTube. My interest in art returned and greatly grew. I started making pencil sketches and drawings of my favorite fictional characters and real-life things while applying the concepts and techniques I have learned through the videos I watched.

Then I decided that I wanted to add color to my art, so I tried experimenting with oil pastels, colored pencils, digital art, watercolor, and gouache. The last two were the ones that I enjoyed using the most which is why I still use them in my current art pieces.

I’m not a professional artist, but I think I do have experience to call myself more than a beginner. As an aspiring artist myself, my goal is to help other aspiring artists have immediate access to these helpful pieces of information, tips, and tricks that helped me become the artist I am today.
